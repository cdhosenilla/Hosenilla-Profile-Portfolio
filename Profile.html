About Me

My name is Carl Daren Hosenilla. I'm 22 years old. My house is in the southeast section of Poblacion, Belison, where all the fisherfolks are, and I am not married. I study as a BS Information Technology student at University of Antique, and I get home every day by 8pm at the latest. I don't smoke, but I occasionally drink. I'm in bed by 11 PM. After having a glass of warm milk and doing about twenty minutes of stretches before going to bed, I usually have no problems sleeping until morning. Just like a baby, I wake up without any fatigue or stress in the morning. I was told there were no issues at my last check-up. I'm trying to explain that I'm a person who wishes to live a very quiet life. I take care not to trouble myself with any enemies, like winning and losing, that would cause me to lose sleep at night. That is how I deal with society, and I know that is what brings me happiness.
